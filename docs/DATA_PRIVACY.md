# Data Privacy and Legal Guardrails

## POPIA Principles
- Minimize collection of personal information.
- Prefer company-level procurement data over individual-level data.
- Redact or hash any direct identifiers in evidence exports.

## Publication Guardrails
- Label outputs as suspected irregularity pending investigation.
- Require at least two corroborating sources before publication.
- Apply legal review before external release.

## Audit and Retention
- Preserve chain-of-custody metadata for uploaded evidence.
- Store transformation logs with source reference and model version.
- Purge non-essential raw artifacts based on retention policy.
